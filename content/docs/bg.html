Category: Process Management
Definition: a job control command that makes sure suspended jobs are not brought to the foreground when resumed. The job indicated with the bg command is the job you want to run in the background. OS systems must have bg to be POSIX complaint

Options Explanation
%, %+, %% refers to the current job
%- or refers to the previous job
%(num) refers to the job number
i.e. %1 or %2

Examples:

bg %1

    resumes job 1 in the background

bg %+

    same as bg % or bg%%

    resumes current job in background
