Category: Process Management
Definition: a job control command that makes sure suspended jobs are not brought to the foreground when resumed. The job indicated with the bg command is the job you want to run in the background.

See: jobs

Options:

  • job
    • %, %+, %%
      • refers to the current job
    • %- or -
      • refers to the previous job
    • 1, 2, 3
      • refers to the job number
      • i.e. %1 or %2
